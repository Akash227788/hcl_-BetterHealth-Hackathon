Features

Online slot booking

In this stage a consumer books a slot in which he/she can collect the ordered products from the shop.

Virtual Queue

A virtual queue is made in which consumers can select a desired slot to watch their products being packed according to the slots available with that shop.

Packaging live stream

An customer centric approach in delivering goods. For customer satisfaction and safety a live video is streamed by the seller in a particular slot where in the customer can view their order packed.

Integrated platform

A platform where buyers and sellers can operate. Separate mode for seller and buyer.

Monthly stats for the seller

A seller can view his monthly stats on each and every product listed.

Panic Buying

"Feature under testing" This feature helps in reducing confusions by keeping stock data transparent when needed.
